About Water Warriors

Meet the team behind this sustainable development initiative

Our Mission

Water Warriors is dedicated to raising awareness about water scarcity and promoting sustainable water use through education, engagement, and action. Our mission is to empower individuals with knowledge and tools to reduce water waste and support clean water initiatives globally.

Our Team

Loris

Project Lead & UX designer

Loris focused on developing the educational content for the Water Facts page and oversaw the project's direction. With a background in environmental science, Loris brought expert knowledge about water conservation issues and designed the user interface.

Hector

UI Designer / Developper

Hector created the responsive layout, and developed the parallax underwater animation. Hector's artistic vision brings the water theme to life throughout the site.

Our Approach

We believe that education becomes more effective when it's engaging and interactive. That's why we've created a gamified approach to learning about water conservation, with interactive elements like our water usage calculator and knowledge quizzes.

Project Development

Research

We collected scientific data about water scarcity, conservation methods, and global water initiatives to ensure our content is accurate and informative.

Design

Our design phase focused on creating a visually appealing interface with water-themed elements and ensuring the site is fully responsive for all devices.

Development

We built the site using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, implementing interactive features and animations to enhance user engagement.

Testing

We tested all features across different devices and browsers to ensure a smooth experience for all users.
